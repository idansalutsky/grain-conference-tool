## Design Context

### Users
Two audiences. **Primary:** Grain's field sales / BD reps — working a busy conference
floor on their **phone**, between conversations, and planning their year at a **desk**.
Non-technical. **Secondary (the reviewer):** Grain's head of sales / head of BD — has
seen a hundred generic dashboards and will reject anything that feels like a CRUD admin.
The job: decide which events deserve budget, know exactly who to approach there, capture
a lead in 10 seconds, judge whether a repeat contact is worth closing, and push it to HubSpot.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **sharp, field-ready, trustworthy-with-edge.** It's an operator's instrument
for closers — Grain is a serious cross-border-FX fintech (treasury/finance buyers), so the
tool must feel credible and precise, but it is a *sales weapon*, not a compliance portal.
Confident, fast, a little opinionated. Emotional goals: a rep feels equipped; a sales lead
feels "someone who gets my job built this."

### Aesthetic Direction
**Editorial-utilitarian, light theme — a premium financial briefing crossed with a field
notebook.** Light (conference daylight + phone readability + a sales, not trading-terminal,
audience). Strong typographic hierarchy; data set like a well-typeset table, not card-soup.
Tier / arc / persona states rendered as crisp typographic **stamps** (letterspaced small
caps with a rule), never generic rounded pills. A confident masthead.
- **Type:** display = **Bricolage Grotesque** (characterful, editorial); body = **Hanken
  Grotesk** (clean, warm, legible, tabular figures for scores/dates/money). No Inter/DM/etc.
- **Color (OKLCH):** warm off-white paper ground (not #fff); ink near-black tinted toward
  the brand hue; Grain green (~oklch hue 165) as the rare action/positive accent (10%).
  Semantic states: warming=green-positive, cooling=blue, tire_kicker=clay/amber, flat=neutral.
- **Anti-references:** generic Tailwind SaaS admin; cyan-on-dark; purple gradients; card-soup
  dashboards; rounded-pill badges everywhere; lone-teal-accent on white.

### Design Principles
1. **Editorial, not admin.** Typographic hierarchy and well-set data carry the design; cards
   are used sparingly, never nested, never identical grids.
2. **Field-first.** Mobile is a first-class layout, not a shrink. The capture flow is thumb-sized.
3. **Earned color.** Neutrals tinted toward Grain green; the green itself is rare and means
   "act / positive". Semantic state colors are the only other hues.
4. **Stamps over pills.** Status (tier/arc/persona) reads like a stamped classification.
5. **Quiet motion.** One orchestrated load with staggered reveals; ease-out; no bounce.
